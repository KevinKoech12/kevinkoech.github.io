KIRUI COLLEGE OF ACCOUNTS IS:

Kirui College of Accounts is a potential school that offers a variety of accounting programs, including certificates, diplomas, and degrees. The school is accredited by the Kenya Accountants and Secretaries Examination Board (KASNEB) and the Association of Certified Public Accountants of Kenya (ICPAK). Kirui College of Accounts is committed to providing its students with the knowledge and skills they need to succeed in the accounting profession.
